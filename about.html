About Dream League Soccer 26

Our Story

Dream League Soccer started as a passion project to create the most immersive mobile soccer experience. Since its launch, millions of players worldwide have built their dream teams and competed at the highest levels.

What Makes Us Special

  • ✅ Real players and teams from major leagues worldwide
  • ✅ Advanced AI for realistic match simulations
  • ✅ Constantly updated with real-world player stats
  • ✅ Thriving global community
  • ✅ Regular events and competitions
  • ✅ Fair and balanced gameplay

Our Mission

To provide soccer fans everywhere with an engaging, fair, and fun gaming experience where they can build their dream team and compete with players from around the globe.
