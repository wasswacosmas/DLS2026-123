How to Play

Getting Started

  1. Download the app from App Store or Google Play
  2. Create your account
  3. Build your dream team
  4. Train your players
  5. Play matches and earn rewards

Building Your Team

Start with a base squad and gradually improve it by:

  • Signing players from the transfer market
  • Training existing players to increase their skills
  • Setting the right formation and tactics
  • Managing your team budget wisely

Match Mechanics

Before each match you can:

  • Select your starting eleven
  • Choose your formation (4-3-3, 4-4-2, 4-2-3-1, etc.)
  • Set attacking/defensive tactics
  • Make substitutions during the match

Competitions

  • League: Weekly matches against other teams
  • Cup: Knockout tournaments for big rewards
  • Special Events: Limited-time challenges with exclusive rewards
